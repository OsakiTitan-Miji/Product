HEALTH DETECTION
The Somnus 4D Duoflex AI massage chair is equipped with an advanced health monitoring system that tracks key metrics like fatigue levels, heart rate, blood oxygen, microcirculation, and heart rate variability. With this data, its AI-powered technology designs a personalized massage experience tailored to your body’s needs in the moment.
SONIC PULSE MASSAGE
Discover the latest in 4D and Sonic Massage, where cutting-edge technology meets timeless technique. By blending the deep, rapid pulses of sonic massage with the proven benefits of shiatsu, this innovation delivers a massage experience that’s truly unmatched.
4D + 4D MECHANISM
The advanced 4D+4D dual massage mechanism uses quad precision rollers that adjust in depth and speed for targeted muscle relief. Each mechanism has independent controls that follow your natural contours, while the heated back rollers blend therapeutic warmth with precision massage for unmatched comfort.
FLEX SL-TRACK SYSTEM
The Somnus 4D Duoflex AI redefines the traditional SL-track with its innovative SL Flex Track system,delivering greater flexibility for unmatched adaptability and comfort. Unlike standard tracks, this advanced design stays aligned with the backrest, ensuring continuous support for the entire body from neck and down to the hamstrings.

KNEADING
Unlike typical massage chairs, the Somnus 4D Duoflex AI addresses the calf areas by using deep calf kneading to ease tension, boost circulation, and support muscle recovery.

ZERO GRAVITY
The Somnus 4D Duoflex AI reclines into Zero Gravity position, reducing spinal pressure and enhancing circulation. This position helps you feel weightless, delivering maximum comfort and relaxation during your massage.
