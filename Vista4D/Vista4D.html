Introducing the Osaki Vista 4D, a state-of-the-art massage chair designed to redefine relaxation and elevate everyday comfort. Equipped with an industry-first 53.1” SL-Track system, an advanced 4D massage mechanism, and intelligent automatic body detection, the Vista 4D delivers precise, full-body therapy tailored to your unique needs. With its sleek design, durable build, and user-friendly 7” touchscreen controls, the Vista 4D transforms any space into a personal wellness retreat.

38-CELL FULL BODY
AIR MASSAGE
With 38 air cells strategically placed throughout the chair, the Vista 4D delivers soothing compression therapy that boosts circulation and relieves tension. A three-row foot roller system works with airbags to provide a deep, revitalizing massage for the feet and calves. SL-TRACK
SYSTEM
The Vista 4D features an industry-first 53.1” SL-Track system that follows the natural “S” curve of the human spine. By extending from neck to legs, it provides full-body coverage, promotes spinal alignment, and ensures deeper, more comfortable massage relief.

BODY SCAN
The Vista 4D’s automatic body scan precisely detects the user’s shoulder position and body shape after reclining, ensuring each massage is customized for maximum comfort and effectiveness. By adapting to different body types, it delivers accurate, targeted therapy every time.

MINIMUM DOORWAY
Measuring Guide
  • Calculate the doorway width by subtracting 1 inch from the smallest inner width of the door frame. (1/2 inch from each side)
  • Please use a standard measuring tape to measure the doorway.
  • If the door cannot be fully opened, open it as much as possible and measure the width from the edge of the door hinge.
  • If there is a rounded curve on the wall, calculate the inner width based on the most protruding point of the wall.
