Introducing the Osaki Vista 4D, a state-of-the-art massage chair designed to redefine relaxation and elevate everyday comfort. Equipped with an industry-first 53.1” double rail system, an advanced 4D massage mechanism, and intelligent automatic body detection, the Vista 4D delivers precise, full-body therapy tailored to your unique needs. Enhanced with soothing waist heating, powerful triple foot rollers, convenient wireless charging, and immersive Bluetooth speakers, it offers the perfect balance of innovation and luxury. With its sleek design, durable build, and user-friendly 7” touchscreen controls, the Vista 4D transforms any space into a personal wellness retreat.

Osaki Vista 4D
32-CELL FULL BODY
AIR MASSAGE
With 32 air cells strategically placed throughout the chair, the Vista 4D delivers soothing compression therapy that boosts circulation and relieves tension. A three-row foot roller system works with airbags to provide a deep, revitalizing massage for the feet, calves, and ankles. Advanced carbon fiber waist heating quickly reaches 104–122°F (40–50°C), improving blood flow, easing lower back pain, and offering a comforting “hot stone” effect for deeper relaxation.
DOUBLE CHUTE
GUIDE RAIL
The Vista 4D features an industry-first 53.1” double chute guide rail that follows the natural “S” curve of the human spine. By extending from neck to legs, it provides full-body coverage, promotes spinal alignment, and ensures deeper, more comfortable massage relief.
AUTOMATIC
BODY SCAN
The Vista 4D’s automatic body scan precisely detects the user’s shoulder position and body shape after reclining, ensuring each massage is customized for maximum comfort and effectiveness. By adapting to different body types, it delivers accurate, targeted therapy every time.
4D MASSAGE
MECHANISM
The Vista 4D redefines relaxation with its advanced 4D massage mechanism, delivering precise roller intensity and adaptive speed control. Its intelligent system creates a lifelike, deeply immersive massage tailored to your body’s unique contours.
INTELLIGENT
VOICE MODE
The Vista 4D massage chair elevates your experience with seamless voice command integration. Simply speak preset commands to control the chair effortlessly—no need to lift a finger. A comprehensive list of commands can be found in the manual.
ZERO GRAVITY RECLINING
Inspired by NASA technology, the Vista 4D offers Zero Gravity positioning, raising your legs to heart level to reduce back pressure, enhance circulation, and deliver consistent massage pressure from shoulders to lower back.
SPACE SAVING DESIGN
Using Space-Saving Technology, the Vista 4D can be placed 5.9 inches away from the wall and still achieve a complete recline.
AUTOMATIC
EXTENDABLE DESIGN
The Vista 4D’s footrest extends up to 7.9 inches with a spring-assisted mechanism, accommodation users of various heights for a more customized fit.

Measuring Guide
  • Calculate the doorway width by subtracting 1 inch from the smallest inner width of the door frame. (1/2 inch from each side)
  • Please use a standard measuring tape to measure the doorway.
  • If the door cannot be fully opened, open it as much as possible and measure the width from the edge of the door hinge.
  • If there is a rounded curve on the wall, calculate the inner width based on the most protruding point of the wall.
