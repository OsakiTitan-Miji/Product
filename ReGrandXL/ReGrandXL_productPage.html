3D massage mechanism
The Titan Grande XL features a next-generation robotic 3D massage system. This innovative technology allows the massage heads to move up, down, side-to-side, and in and out from the backrest, enabling precise targeting of specific areas along the back.
Automatic body scan
The Grande XL’s automatic body scan feature customizes each massage by detecting your body shape and contours. This smart technology ensures that the massage heads adjust perfectly to target your unique pressure points for a personalized, effective experience.
3-STAGE ZERO GRAVITY
The Titan Grande XL, inspired by NASA technology, features Zero Gravity positioning that raises your legs to heart level. This position evenly distributes your weight, reducing back pressure and improving leg circulation.
